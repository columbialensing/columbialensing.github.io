Gesamtkunstwerk 1020

Welcome! We are a group of young people who currently live in New York city. We are artists, designers, architects, scientists, and social workers... We share a common desire to exchange ideas, inspire, and be inspired. During each of our events, we invite 10 people from various backgrounds to give a 20 minute talk.
