Gesamtkunstwerk 1020

Welcome! We are a group of young people who currently live in the New York city. We are artists, designers, architects, scientists, social workers... one thing we have in common is that we want to share our ideas, inspire, and be inspired. During each of our event, we invite 10 people from various backgrounds to give a 20 minute talk.
